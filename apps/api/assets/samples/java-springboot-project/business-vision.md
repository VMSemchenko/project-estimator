# Business Vision: Healthcare Patient Portal

## Overview

Build a secure patient portal for healthcare providers that enables patients to access their medical records, schedule appointments, communicate with healthcare providers, and manage prescriptions online.

## Goals

- Provide patients 24/7 access to their health information
- Streamline appointment scheduling and reduce no-shows
- Enable secure messaging between patients and providers
- Support prescription refill requests and medication tracking
- Ensure HIPAA compliance and data security

## Success Criteria

- Launch within 6 months
- Support 50,000 registered patients
- 99.99% uptime for critical services
- Reduce appointment scheduling calls by 40%
- Patient satisfaction score above 4.2/5

## Target Users

- **Primary**: Patients seeking to manage their healthcare online
- **Secondary**: Healthcare providers (doctors, nurses)
- **Tertiary**: Administrative staff and billing departments

## Key Features

1. Patient registration and identity verification
2. Medical records access (lab results, visit summaries)
3. Appointment scheduling and reminders
4. Secure messaging with healthcare team
5. Prescription management and refill requests
6. Bill pay and insurance information
7. Telehealth video consultations
8. Health tracking and wellness goals

## Business Constraints

- Budget: $500,000
- Timeline: 6 months to MVP
- Team: 4 backend developers, 3 frontend developers, 2 QA, 2 DevOps, 1 Security Engineer
- Must comply with HIPAA, HITECH, and state privacy laws
- Must pass security audit before launch

## Risks and Mitigations

| Risk                         | Probability | Impact   | Mitigation                                   |
| ---------------------------- | ----------- | -------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| HIPAA compliance issues      | Medium      | Critical | Engage compliance consultant early           |
| Data breach                  | Low         | Critical | Security-first design, encryption everywhere |
| Integration with EHR systems | High        | High     | Use standard protocols (HL7 FHIR)            |
| User adoption resistance     | Medium      | Medium   | User research, intuitive design              |
