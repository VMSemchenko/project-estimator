# Business Vision: E-Commerce Platform

## Overview
Build a modern e-commerce platform for selling digital products. The platform will enable customers to browse, purchase, and download digital products including software, e-books, courses, and digital art.

## Goals
- Enable digital product sales with seamless user experience
- Support multiple payment methods for global accessibility
- Provide customer analytics for business insights
- Enable content creators to publish and sell their digital products
- Ensure secure and reliable transactions

## Success Criteria
- Launch within 3 months
- Support 10,000 concurrent users
- 99.9% uptime
- Process transactions under 3 seconds
- Customer satisfaction score above 4.5/5

## Target Users
- **Primary**: Digital content creators looking to sell their products
- **Secondary**: Customers seeking digital products
- **Tertiary**: Business analysts tracking sales metrics

## Key Features
1. User registration and authentication
2. Product catalog with categories and search
3. Shopping cart and checkout
4. Multiple payment gateway integration
5. Digital product delivery system
6. Customer dashboard and order history
7. Seller dashboard and analytics
8. Admin panel for platform management

## Business Constraints
- Budget: $150,000
- Timeline: 3 months to MVP
- Team: 2 frontend developers, 2 backend developers, 1 QA, 1 DevOps
- Must comply with PCI-DSS for payment processing
- Must support GDPR for European customers

## Risks and Mitigations
| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Payment integration delays | Medium | High | Start integration early, have backup provider |
| Performance issues at scale | Medium | High | Load testing, auto-scaling infrastructure |
| Security vulnerabilities | Low | Critical | Security audit, penetration testing |
